TrendyT

Project Date Mar 2022
Languages JavaScript, JSX, HTML, CSS
Libraries React, React Router, Redux
Visit https://www.trendyt.com

I developed the TrendyT website using React, a powerful JavaScript library known for its flexibility and performance. This dynamic web application comprises four main sections: Home, Products, About, and Contact pages, ensuring a seamless user experience. Additionally, I implemented essential user functionalities, including Login, Signup, Cart, and Checkout pages, to enhance user engagement and shopping convenience. One of the standout features of the TrendyT website is its product filtering system, allowing users to easily browse and discover items in specific categories, such as Men's Clothing, Women's Clothing, Jewelry, and Electronics. This intuitive filtering mechanism streamlines the shopping process and ensures a tailored shopping experience for every user.

The Cart functionality enables users to add desired items for purchase and seamlessly proceed to the Checkout page by simply clicking the checkout button. This straightforward and secure checkout process enhances user satisfaction and encourages successful transactions. Moreover, TrendyT incorporates a personalized touch with its 'You May Also Like' feature. When users click on a product, they are presented with a curated selection of related items, enhancing product discovery and encouraging users to explore additional offerings.
